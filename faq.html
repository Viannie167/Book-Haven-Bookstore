Common Questions

What are your hours of operation?

We are open Monday through Friday, 9 AM - 8 PM.

Saturday: 10 AM to 6 PM.

Sunday: 11 AM to 5 PM.

How do I get a library card?

You can sign up for a library card at our front desk or fill out an online registration form on our website.

Do you offer ebooks or audiobooks?

Yes! We offer a wide range at checkout.

What is the late fee policy?

We do not charge late fees! However, we encourage the timely return of materials so others may enjoy them.

Do you offer free Wi-Fi?

Yes, free Wi-Fi is available throughout the bookstore. Look for the network name BookHavenGuest.
